Coding Challenges

Canvas

  • Background Scrolling - Can you figure out how to get a background image to continously scroll across the canvas?
  • Sprite Animation - Can you make the zombie sprite look like it is walking?
  • Parallax Scrolling - A parallax scrolling effect gives the perception of depth by making things closer to the viewer scroll at a faster rate than things that are farther away from the viewer. Can you simulate depth using four different layers of clouds scroll at different rates?
